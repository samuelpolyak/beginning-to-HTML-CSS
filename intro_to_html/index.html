The Best Food In Ukraine

Ukraine is a country with many delicous food. Foods that give you comfort and begging for more and more servings. People who have tried Ukrainian food will always tell you about how enjoyable the food is. And that you should go and try it the amazing food for yourself.

Types Of Foods

Ukraines food is mouth-watering. With foods like borscht, Varenyky, Holubtsi, Deruni, Olivier Potato Salad, Nalysnyky, Salo, Chicken Kyiv, Pierogi and Paska bread. Take a bite of any of these foods and you will be hooked on the savory flavor.

Random Facts

  • Borsht has beans, beets, carrots, potatoes, and even sometimes cabbage inside of it making it super healthy with protein, iron, carotene, and potassium.
  • Fun fact about Salo is that it helped Ukrainian peasants in surviving foreign invasions and having low supply of food.
  • Ukrainian diet include potatoes, grains, beets, cabbage, fruits, and berries.
  • The grains in Ukraine are some of the best in all of Europe so it is no wonder that their bakers are masterful craftsmen as well.
